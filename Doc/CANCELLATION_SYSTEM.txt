================================================================================
  CANCELLATION SYSTEM — HOW IT WORKS
  BookingSystem v1.0
================================================================================


OVERVIEW
--------
When a student cancels a booked lesson, the system automatically calculates
what happens to the deposit based on how much notice was given. A warning
dialog always appears BEFORE the cancellation is committed, so the student
sees exactly what they will lose or get back.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT HAPPENS STEP BY STEP (STUDENT)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Student clicks "Cancel" on a booked slot.

2. The system immediately calculates:
   - How many hours remain until the lesson starts
   - What the cancellation tier is (see Tiers below)
   - How much deposit is held in escrow
   - How much will be refunded vs. kept by the teacher

3. A warning dialog appears showing:
   - Lesson details (teacher, time, date)
   - Time remaining: e.g. "2d 4h remaining"
   - Cancellation outcome: e.g. "Full Refund" / "Partial Refund" / "No Refund"
   - Exact euro amounts: e.g. "€9.00 will be refunded"
   - [Cancel — go back]  [Confirm cancellation]

4. If the student confirms, the slot is staged (amber/pending) — NOT yet final.

5. When the student clicks SAVE/DONE, the cancellation is committed:
   - Slot is freed (available for others to book)
   - Deposit is refunded or kept according to the tier
   - Student wallet is updated immediately
   - Navbar balance updates instantly
   - Teacher receives an email notification


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CANCELLATION TIERS (default policy)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The cancellation window is set per teacher in their profile.
Default settings:
  - Window:         48 hours
  - Partial keeps:  50% of deposit
  - Strict mode:    OFF

TIER 1 — FULL REFUND
  Condition:  Cancellation > 48h before lesson
  Student:    Gets 100% of deposit back
  Teacher:    Gets nothing
  Example:    Deposit €9.00 → Refund €9.00

TIER 2 — PARTIAL REFUND
  Condition:  Cancellation between 24h and 48h before lesson
  Student:    Gets 50% of deposit back (configurable)
  Teacher:    Keeps 50% of deposit
  Example:    Deposit €9.00 → Refund €4.50, Teacher keeps €4.50

TIER 3 — NO REFUND (Forfeit)
  Condition:  Cancellation < 24h before lesson, or lesson already started
  Student:    Gets nothing back
  Teacher:    Keeps 100% of deposit
  Example:    Deposit €9.00 → Teacher keeps €9.00

TIER 4 — TEACHER/ADMIN CANCEL
  Condition:  The teacher or admin cancels (any time)
  Student:    Always gets 100% of deposit back
  Teacher:    Gets nothing
  Note:       Teacher bears full responsibility for their own cancellations

TIER 5 — NO ESCROW
  Condition:  No deposit was paid (cash_on_site payment mode, or deposit waived)
  Student:    Nothing to refund — slot is simply freed
  Teacher:    Nothing affected
  Note:       Slot is cancelled without any financial transaction


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT HAPPENS WHEN TEACHER CANCELS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Teacher stages the cancellation in their schedule.
2. When saved:
   - Slot is freed
   - Student deposit is ALWAYS fully refunded (regardless of notice period)
   - Student wallet updates immediately
   - Email sent to student

Teachers always bear full responsibility. There is no penalty tier for them —
only the student's deposit protection applies.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ADMIN OVERRIDES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Admins can:
  - Release any escrow at any time (overrides all policy)
  - View all escrows in the Payments tab
  - See deposit status: unpaid / held / refund_requested / refunded / forfeited

Admin cancellations always trigger a full refund to the student.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TEACHER PROFILE — POLICY SETTINGS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Teachers can configure their cancellation policy in their profile under
the Deposit section:

  Cancellation window (hours)
    How many hours before a lesson the "full refund" window closes.
    Default: 48 hours
    Example: Set to 72 = student gets full refund if they cancel 3+ days ahead

  Partial refund at cancellation (% of deposit)
    How much of the deposit the student keeps in the partial tier.
    Default: 50% (student gets half back)
    Example: Set to 0 = student gets nothing even in the middle tier
    Example: Set to 100 = student always gets full deposit back (lenient policy)

  Strict mode (checkbox)
    If ON: no refund is ever given to the student, regardless of notice.
    Use for: teachers with high demand / fully booked schedules.
    Default: OFF


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ESCROW — HOW MONEY IS HELD
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

When a student books a lesson (instant payment mode):
  1. Deposit amount is calculated from teacher profile
  2. Deposit is deducted from student's wallet immediately
  3. Deposit is held in ESCROW (neither student nor teacher can spend it)
  4. Escrow status: "held"

When cancellation is processed:
  - Full/partial refund: escrow → back to student wallet
  - Forfeit: escrow → transferred to teacher wallet
  - No escrow: nothing happens

When lesson is confirmed (student confirms lesson happened):
  - Deposit is released to teacher
  - Escrow status: "released"

ESCROW STATES:
  unpaid           → deposit not yet paid
  held             → deposit deducted from student, sitting in escrow
  refund_requested → student/teacher initiated refund
  refunded         → money returned to student wallet
  forfeited        → money transferred to teacher wallet
  released         → lesson confirmed, money released to teacher


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PAYMENT MODES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

INSTANT (default)
  Deposit is charged at booking time.
  Cancellation policy applies fully.

CASH ON SITE
  No money moves at booking time.
  Cancellation frees the slot only — no financial transactions.
  Cancellation policy does not apply (nothing to refund or forfeit).


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SUMMARY TABLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Notice          | Student gets   | Teacher gets  | Applies when
  ----------------|----------------|---------------|------------------
  > 48h           | Full refund    | Nothing       | Default tier 1
  24h – 48h       | 50% refund     | 50% deposit   | Default tier 2
  < 24h           | Nothing        | Full deposit  | Default tier 3
  Teacher cancels | Full refund    | Nothing       | Always
  Cash on site    | N/A            | N/A           | No escrow
  No deposit paid | N/A            | N/A           | No escrow
